River Raid in p5.js

How to Play:

  • Use LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move the plane
  • Press SPACEBAR to shoot
  • Avoid colliding with the river banks
  • Destroy enemies to score points
  • Collect fuel to keep flying

Game Elements

Helicopter - Your player
Fuel Tank - Collect to refuel
Enemy - Avoid or shoot them
Bridge - Shoot to score points
